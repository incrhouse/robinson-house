Getting ready

All you need to get started

Telehealth is simpler than you think. Here's everything you need for a successful session.

📱

A device

Any smartphone, tablet, or computer with a camera and microphone. No special software or downloads required.

🌐

Internet connection

A stable WiFi or cellular connection. Standard home broadband or 4G/5G is more than sufficient.

🔒

A private space

A quiet, private location where you feel comfortable speaking openly. Your car, bedroom, or home office all work well.

📋

Completed intake forms

You'll receive intake paperwork through SimplePractice before your first session. Takes about 15 minutes to complete.

📍

Be in a licensed state

You must be physically located in TX, NM, FL, ID, or NE at the time of your session for Dr. Robinson to legally provide services.

⏰

Show up on time

Sessions start on time. Log in 2-3 minutes early to test your camera and microphone so you don't lose session time.

Telehealth FAQ

Research consistently shows that telehealth therapy produces outcomes equivalent to in-person therapy for most mental health conditions including anxiety, depression, and trauma. Many clients actually find it easier to open up from the comfort of their own space.

Many insurance plans now cover telehealth therapy at the same rate as in-person sessions. Contact your insurance provider to verify your telehealth benefits. Self-pay and sliding scale options are also available.
