About Dr. Robinson

A clinician who also builds the tools he wishes existed

Dr. Cleveland Robinson is a Licensed Professional Counselor-Supervisor (LPC-S) based in Dallas, TX — independently licensed to practice in Texas, New Mexico, Florida, Idaho, and Nebraska. Since 2014, he has provided a full-spectrum behavioral health services to individuals, couples, families, and organizations, with a particular focus on underserved and culturally diverse communities.

Beyond his clinical practice, Dr. Robinson builds free technology tools to extend access to mental health support. Tiyare Care, his AI-powered pediatric triage tool, is used by parents across the DFW area and is built on AAP and Schmitt-Thompson 2026 protocols.
